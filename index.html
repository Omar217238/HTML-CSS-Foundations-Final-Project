Olympics Sports!

The Summer Olympic Games, also known as the Summer Olympics or the Games of the Olympiad, is a major international multi-sport event normally held once every four years.

Some Of The Most Well Known Sports

Football is a dynamic team sport where players aim to score goals by kicking a ball into the opponent's net.

Basketball is a fast-paced game where teams compete to shoot a ball through the opposing team's hoop.

Swimming is an individual or team water sport focused on racing through various strokes in a pool.

Running is a solo endurance sport that tests speed and stamina across short or long distances.

For athletes, the Olympic Games are the ultimate test of spirit, strength, and sacrifice.
-Sebastian Coe, Olympic gold medalist and former head of the London 2012 Olympics
